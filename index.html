It was Merlin who bred the first Dragonkin: strong; fast; smart; beautiful. Subtle at first so nobody really noticed, but as time passed it became clear that these creatures were unlike anyother.

Humans, threatened by their presence, tried to eliminate them. The Dragonkins, however, fought back. Evenutally, the violent impulse of humanking resulted in their ultimate extinction. The Dragonkin however through their intelligence rebuilt civilization from the ground up and in their own image. What they built became known as Tareria.

This is just the beginning.

Welcome to Tareria

These ERC-721 Dragonkin are your key to entering the Tareria universe. You'll need one to mint an avatar but unlike other projects that's not where it ends. Dragonkin will:

  • Automatically upgrade over time. The longer you hold, the rarer the avatar that you can potentially generate.
  • Make you eligible to receive a weapons upgrade token, which can be applied to your avatar.
  • Give you the opportunity to buy real estate in your home city, making you a part of this living, breathing economy.

We fully expect Dragonkin holders to have to make difficult decisions over when and how to use their tokens. Just know that whatever you decide, you're creating something truly unique that will hold real, long term, value.

Enhancements

Gear

Not just words on a screen. These unique ERC-721 tokens allow gear and weapon upgrades that you can choose to apply to your Avatar, giving true customisation and the chance to increase rarity and value.

All holders of Dragonkin on our snapshot date will have the chance to mint one free piece of gear.

Upgrades

Dragonkin doesn't just allow you to mint new avatars - it lets you upgrade too. Every character and accessory has an upgrade pathway, which evolves your artwork and gives you the opportunity to really stand out.

The decision you have to make is when to mint and when to upgrade.

Tareconomy

Tareconomy

Owning an Tareria character doesn't just give you a piece of artwork - it gives you the opportunity to participate in a living, breathing, city-wide economy.

Royalties generated from the buying and selling of Tareria NFTs are converted into $TRIA and go back into the relevant city's economy

.

These are converted into $TRIA tokens and stored in the city's treasury.

Users can form clans with other dragonkins which function as in game DAOs. Users can join these DAOs, go on quests together, and issue clan-specific governance tokens. Rewards earned by quests are distrubuted among memmbers of the clan. Clans allow members to leverage collective in-game experience.

FAQ's

How do I buy a Dragonkin?
You'll be able to mint one directly on the Teraria website over the next few days. Stay Tuned in Discord.
What are your secondary royalty fees?
2.5%
When and how can I buy $TRIA?
Users who purchased a dragonkin will be whitelisted to participate in the ICO of $TRIA during phase 2. Following the conclusion of the private sale, anyone will be able to purchase $TRIA from a dex.
The Roadmap looks incredible. How long will all this take?
Tareria isn't a short term project but, equally, we're not going to make you wait forever. Minting will go online in early December, with the snapshot taking place a couple of weeks later. From there we're into Phase 2, which includes the launch of the game and its in-game currency, 4 - 6 weeks later. Phase 3 is planned for Q1 2022.

One important thing to note is that we will not rush this. Creating a token is easy but doing it in a way that is legally compliant and adds real long term value is far more complex. We're determined to do this right and will take the time needed.
How do avatar upgrades work?
We'll explain more soon but in essence Dragonkin upgrades itself over time, moving through the various levels. When minting you have higher chance at receiving an upgraded avatar but when upgrading this is guaranteed. So, do you use a single Dragonkin on your avatar or do you use two (one to mint and one to upgrade?).

Mixed into this decision is also the fact that there is some incredible artwork even at the lower levels, so it's up to you to decide when to twist and when to stick.
